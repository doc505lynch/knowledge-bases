# Required Bankruptcy Forms: Federal and California Local

## Overview

Bankruptcy petitions require a comprehensive set of forms disclosing all assets, debts, income, expenses, and financial history. Forms are divided into:

1. **Official Bankruptcy Forms** — Federal forms required in all U.S. bankruptcy courts
2. **California Local Forms** — Additional forms required by individual California bankruptcy districts

All Official Forms: **www.uscourts.gov/forms/bankruptcy-forms**

---

## Required Official Forms — Chapter 7 Individual Filings

### Petition and Core Documents

| Form | Name | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| **Form 101** | Voluntary Petition | Core filing document; lists basic debtor info, prior cases, chapter type |
| **Form 101A** | Initial Statement About Eviction Judgment | Required if you have a pending eviction with pre-petition judgment |
| **Form 101B** | Statement About Payment of Eviction Judgment | Follow-up eviction certification |

### Schedules (All Required)

| Form | Schedule | What It Lists |
|------|----------|---------------|
| **Form 106A/B** | Property | All real and personal property you own or have an interest in |
| **Form 106C** | Exemptions | Property you claim as exempt, citing the specific California statute (System 1 or 2) |
| **Form 106D** | Secured Creditors | All creditors with liens (mortgage lenders, car lenders) |
| **Form 106E/F** | Unsecured Creditors | Priority unsecured (taxes, support) and general unsecured (credit cards, medical) |
| **Form 106G** | Executory Contracts and Unexpired Leases | Active contracts and leases (apartment, car, service) |
| **Form 106H** | Codebtors | Anyone who is a co-signer or co-borrower on any of your debts |
| **Form 106I** | Income | Current monthly income from all sources |
| **Form 106J** | Expenses | Current monthly living expenses |
| **Form 106J-2** | Expenses for Separate Household | Required if you maintain a separate household |

### Summary and Declarations

| Form | Name | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| **Form 106Sum** | Summary of Assets and Liabilities | Summary page totaling all schedules |
| **Form 106Dec** | Declaration About Schedules | Signed declaration of accuracy under penalty of perjury |
| **Form 107** | Statement of Financial Affairs | Detailed financial history: income (2 years), payments to creditors (90 days/1 year for insiders), lawsuits, property transfers |

### Income and Means Test Forms

| Form | Name | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| **Form 122A-1** | Chapter 7 Statement of Current Monthly Income | Calculates average monthly income for Means Test |
| **Form 122A-1Supp** | Statement of Exemption from Presumption of Abuse | For disabled veterans or primarily-business-debt filers |
| **Form 122A-2** | Chapter 7 Means Test Calculation | Full calculation if income exceeds median |

### Additional Required Documents

| Document | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Credit Counseling Certificate | Within 180 days of filing, from approved agency |
| Pay Stubs | Copies from the 60 days before filing |
| Tax Returns | Most recent year's federal return; trustee may request 2 years |
| Photo ID and Social Security Card | Required at the 341 Meeting |

## Required Official Forms — Chapter 13 Individual Filings

All schedules above (106A/B through 106J, 107, 106Sum, 106Dec) plus:

| Form | Name | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| **Form 113** | Chapter 13 Plan | Proposed repayment plan — due within 14 days of petition (local form may be required instead) |
| **Form 122C-1** | Chapter 13 Statement of Current Monthly Income | Determines plan length (3 vs. 5 years) |
| **Form 122C-2** | Chapter 13 Calculation of Disposable Income | Calculates monthly payment if income exceeds median |

### Fee Waivers (Chapter 7 Only)
Filers below **150% of the federal poverty line** who cannot pay in installments may apply for a complete fee waiver using **Official Form 103B**.

### Installment Payments
All filers may pay in up to **4 installments** within 120 days of filing using **Official Form 103A**.
