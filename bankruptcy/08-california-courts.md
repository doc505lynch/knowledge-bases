# California Bankruptcy Courts: Districts, Locations, and Local Rules

## Overview

California is divided into **four federal bankruptcy court districts**, each operating within the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals. Each district has its own Local Bankruptcy Rules (LBRs), judges, preferred forms, and filing procedures.

---

## Northern District of California (CANB)

**Website:** www.canb.uscourts.gov

### Counties Covered
Alameda, Contra Costa, Del Norte, Humboldt, Lake, Marin, Mendocino, Monterey, Napa, San Benito, San Francisco, San Mateo, Santa Clara, Santa Cruz, Solano, Sonoma

### Divisional Offices

| Division | Address |
|----------|---------|
| **San Francisco** | 450 Golden Gate Ave., Mail Box 36099, San Francisco, CA 94102 |
| **Oakland** | Ronald V. Dellums Federal Bldg., 1301 Clay St., Rm. 300N, Oakland, CA 94612 |
| **San Jose** | 280 S. 1st St., Rm. 3035, San Jose, CA 95113 |
| **Santa Rosa** | 99 South E Street, Santa Rosa, CA 95404 |

### Key Local Rules
- Chapter 13 plans must use the court's model plan format (NDC-1)
- Mandatory self-calendaring system for motions
- Electronic filing required for attorneys; paper filing allowed for pro se debtors
- **Self-Help Center** at San Jose and Oakland divisions (free, staffed by volunteer attorneys)

## Central District of California (CACB)

**Website:** www.cacb.uscourts.gov

**This is the largest bankruptcy court in the United States by case volume.**

### Counties Covered
Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside, San Bernardino, San Luis Obispo, Santa Barbara, Ventura

### Divisional Offices

| Division | Address | Counties Served |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| **Los Angeles** | Edward R. Roybal Federal Building, 255 E. Temple St., Los Angeles, CA 90012 | Los Angeles County (primary) |
| **San Fernando Valley** | 21041 Burbank Blvd., Woodland Hills, CA 91367 | Northwest Los Angeles County |
| **Santa Ana** | Ronald Reagan Federal Bldg., 411 W. 4th St., Santa Ana, CA 92701 | Orange County |
| **Riverside** | 3420 Twelfth St., Riverside, CA 92501 | Riverside and San Bernardino |
| **Santa Barbara** | 1415 State St., Santa Barbara, CA 93101 | San Luis Obispo, Santa Barbara, Ventura |

### Key Local Rules
- Local Bankruptcy Rules (LBRs) are among the most detailed in California
- Chapter 13 must use local plan form **F 3015-1** (not Official Form 113)
- All attorneys must file electronically via CM/ECF
- **Self-Calendaring** required for most motions via the court's online scheduling system
- **Self-Help Center** at Los Angeles (255 E. Temple St., Suite 1260) and Santa Ana (411 W. 4th St., Suite 1-104)

## Southern District of California (CASB)

**Website:** www.casb.uscourts.gov

### Counties Covered
San Diego and Imperial

### Office

| Location | Address |
|----------|---------|
| **San Diego** | Jacob Weinberger U.S. Courthouse, 325 West F Street, San Diego, CA 92101 |

### Key Local Rules
- Chapter 13 plan must comply with LBR 3015-1 and use the district's model plan
- Mandatory mediation for certain Chapter 11 matters
- Paper filing available for pro se debtors; attorneys must file electronically

## Finding Your District

File in the district where you maintained your **domicile, principal place of business, or principal assets** for the greater part of the **180 days immediately before filing**.

| Location | District |
|----------|---------|
| Los Angeles / Orange / Riverside / San Bernardino | Central District |
| San Diego / Imperial | Southern District |
| Sacramento / Fresno / San Joaquin Valley | Eastern District |
| San Francisco Bay Area / Monterey Bay / North Coast | Northern District |
